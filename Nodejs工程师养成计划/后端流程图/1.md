  SubmitFeedback --> FeedbackStatus[查看反馈状态]

  Dashboard --> ApplyArticle[申请发布文章]

  FillForm --> SubmitArticle[提交审核]
  SubmitArticle --> AwaitApproval[等待后台审核]

  CollectArticle --> PersonalCenter[信息中心 - 收藏管理]
  FeedbackStatus --> PersonalCenter
  AwaitApproval --> PersonalCenter

  PersonalCenter --> End([结束])
